| Intuitive

Click on the image to enlarge.

CCPatents separates the process of generating search string into entering keywords - entering synonyms - generating search string, tagging all words so that users can focus only on words.

If you press the Patents Search button, CCPatents will send the search string to the search service you selected and display the search results automatically.

And instead of traditional field-specific input methods, CCPatents adopted keyword-specific input method.

| Trademark search

Click on the image to enlarge.

Do you know search string is also used for trademark search?

You must search text trademark, having the widest range of rights, with homonyms because similar sounds can be an infringement.

CCPatents can generate a trademark search string for the KIPRIS, and the results are immediately displayed in the browser.

※ KIPRIS is a patent search service that Korea Intellectual Property Office operates, providing trademark search service of major countries.

| Thesaurus

Click on the image to enlarge.

CCPatents has built-in WordNet and KIPRIS thesaurus, but the synonyms you want may not be found automatically, or words you don't need may be found. In this case, you can add or delete words manually.

Added synonyms are automatically stored in 'My Thesaurus' and can be automatically retrieved in the next search.

On the other hand, deleted words are automatically stored in the 'Filter Thesaurus' and can be automatically blocked in the next search.

| Various platforms

Patent offices in major countries, including the United States, Europe, and Korea, provide patent search service.

In the US, it is USPTO, and in Europe, it is Espacenet.

In private enterprises, Google patents provide about 9 billion patent databases of 17 patent offices.

CCPatents can change and generate search string of the service you select based on the words entered with a few clicks.

It also automatically considers rules that apply differently for each service and alerts you.

| Advanced history

Click on the image to enlarge.

Search string that find results with many similar patents isn't created at once.

And you might have to search for the invention separately.

CCPatents can automatically save search string and provide the features to comment and rate each search string.

In addition, it provides restore, copy, search again, and more.
